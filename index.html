Modular Script System: GameObjectBase

At the heart of MILEHIGH.WORLD's scalability and maintainability is the GameObjectBase class. All in-game entities inherit from this foundational class, ensuring a consistent structure and a unified approach to handling game logic and data.

Purpose & Benefits:

  • Consistency: Ensures all entities share common properties and behaviors.
  • Scalability: Easily add new entity types without rewriting core logic.
  • Maintainability: Centralized updates to base functionalities propagate across all derived classes.
  • Modularity: Encourages breaking down complex systems into smaller, manageable components.
